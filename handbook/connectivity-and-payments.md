# 網路與支付

**Status:** Guidance with official links — **not** a claim of current card benefits.  
**Updated:** 2026-07-21 Before-Trip Textbook

## Connectivity

Options typically include eSIM, physical SIM, or portable Wi-Fi. Choose at T-30 after comparing:

- Coverage in Seoul + Busan  
- Hotspot needs for two phones  
- Setup friction at arrival (tired + luggage)  

Install and test eSIM before departure when possible. Keep a physical-SIM contingency if one phone fails setup.

## Payments

- Cards are widely accepted in cities; small vendors may prefer cash.  
- Carry a modest KRW cash buffer.  
- Prefer charging in **KRW**; **decline DCC** (dynamic currency conversion) when the terminal offers TWD conversion at a poor rate.

## Apple Pay

- Acceptance varies by merchant and card network. Do not assume 100% coverage; always have a physical card.

## Taiwan banks (Taishin / Fubon / Richart-class products)

- Overseas fees, FX markup, and travel notices **change**.  
- Before trip, read your card’s official pages only (Taishin / Fubon official sites).  
- This handbook intentionally **does not** quote cashback rates or “best card” claims.
