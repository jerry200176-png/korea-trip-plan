# 特殊體驗

## 韓服

- Pair with palace / bukchon morning (Day 2 skeleton).
- Confirm rental duration, shoes, hair, and photo rules per shop.
- Reserve only after dates lock; store confirmation offline.

## 韓國算命

- Language support varies widely — verify Chinese/English before paying.
- Choose reputable storefronts; treat prices as provisional until quoted.
- Emotional framing: fun shared memory, not life directives.

## 海灘與風景

- Haeundae / Gwangalli are Provisional anchors for Busan days.
- March sea is often windy/cool — dress for wind, not summer beachwear norms.

## 拍照

- Target ~80% couple photos.
- Instant camera: spare film; avoid leaving film in hot cars/bags; keep a digital backup of favorites if scanned later.

## 拍立得注意

- Cold can affect development — warm pocket briefly if needed (follow film brand guidance).
- Do not stack freshly ejected shots tightly until dry.
