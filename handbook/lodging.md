# 住宿選擇原則

**Status:** Criteria only — no hotels booked or endorsed as Confirmed.

## Must-haves

- Clean and comfortable
- **Double bed** (명시 더블/킹 — verify photos + room type name)
- Safe evening access
- Manageable transit to that day’s areas / to airport or KTX

## Nice-to-haves

- Convenience store within a short flat walk
- Reasonable soundproofing
- Heating for March nights
- Luggage space for shopping expansion
- Clear cancellation policy until dates are firm

## Area logic (Provisional route)

### Seoul (4N provisional)

Prefer areas that reduce daily cross-city travel for palace/shopping days (e.g. Jongno / City Hall / Myeongdong belt **or** a single well-connected hub). Exact neighborhood shortlist after D2.

### Busan (2N provisional for 7-day trip)

Prefer Haeundae or near subway that reaches beach with few transfers — with only two nights, stay beach-adjacent to avoid wasting the single full Busan day on transfers.

## Ranking rule

Do **not** sort only by lowest price. Score: sleep quality, bed type, transit, cancellation, walking load from station.
