# 天氣與備案

**Source:** KMA [`src-kma`](https://www.kma.go.kr). Historical climate ≠ forecast.

## March (Assumption month)

- Typically cool; mornings/evenings colder; coastal wind in Busan.
- Pack layers + wind/waterproof shell.
- Exact forecast: only trustworthy within ~T-7.

## Contingencies

| Condition | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Rain | Swap to indoor shopping / cafes; cut Bukchon slopes |
| Cold snap | Shorten outdoor blocks; taxi upgrades OK |
| Poor air quality | Prefer indoor; reduce long outdoor photoshoots |
| Strong coastal wind | Skip long beach walks; viewpoint photo only |

## Clothing philosophy

Comfort and safety first. No unhealthy appearance standards for cold weather.
