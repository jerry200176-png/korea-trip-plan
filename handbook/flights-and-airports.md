**Status:** Provisional framework — airports not Founder-locked (D3).  
**Sources:** Incheon / Gimpo / Gimhae official sites (`src-incheon`, `src-gmp`, `src-pusan-air`).

## Taiwan departure options

Common options include **TPE (Taoyuan)** and **TSA (Songshan)**. Availability depends on airline and season — compare official airline timetables after D1/D3.

## Korea arrival options

| Airport | Code | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| Incheon | ICN | Main international gateway for Seoul; longer transfer into city |
| Gimpo | GMP | Closer to central Seoul for some domestic/international routes |
| Gimhae (Busan) | PUS | Natural if itinerary ends or starts in Busan |

**Do not** assume open-jaw vs round-trip yet.

## Airport → lodging

- Prefer official airport railroad / limousine bus pages for last-train and operating hours.
- With luggage + tired feet: taxi may be worth the upgrade on arrival night.
- Record last-transit times into Day 1 after lodging is chosen.

## Liquids & baggage

- Follow **your airline** + departure airport security rules for liquids/batteries.
- Korea Customs separately regulates what you may bring in (`src-customs-kr`).

## Tax refund & departure buffer

- Tax refund desks and procedures change; use airport official guides near travel time.
- Departure day: use airline-recommended airport arrival time; dual-city trips ending in Busan need PUS-specific timing.

## Status discipline

Until tickets exist: all flight blocks remain `Provisional` / `DecisionRequired`. Never paste PNRs into git.
