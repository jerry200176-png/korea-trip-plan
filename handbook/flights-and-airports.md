## Taiwan departure options

Common options include **TPE (Taoyuan)** and **TSA (Songshan)**. Availability depends on airline and season — compare official airline timetables after D1.

## Korea arrival options

| Airport | Code | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| Incheon | ICN | Main international gateway for Seoul; AREX / limousine / taxi teaching on `/transport/` |
| Gimpo | GMP | Closer to central Seoul for some routes; not assumed for this trip |

**Do not** assume open-jaw vs round-trip yet.

## Airport → lodging (teaching)

- Prefer official airport railroad / limousine pages for products and operating windows.
- VisitKorea frames AREX as a fast ICN–Seoul Station link; limousine needs advance tickets.
- With luggage + tired feet: taxi may be the Low-Energy Rescue on arrival night.
- Record last-transit times into Day 1 **after** lodging is chosen.

## Liquids & baggage

- Follow **your airline** + departure airport security rules for liquids/batteries.
- Korea Customs separately regulates what you may bring in.

## Tax refund & departure buffer

- Tax refund desks and procedures change; use airport official guides near travel time.
- Departure day: use airline-recommended airport arrival time; dual-city trips ending in Busan need PUS-specific timing (normal / luggage / taxi Rescue versions).

## Status discipline

Until tickets exist: all flight blocks remain Provisional / DecisionRequired. Never paste PNRs into git. Never invent 2027 schedules as current booking facts.
