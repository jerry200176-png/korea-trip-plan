# 入境與法規

**Status:** Research snapshot 2026-07-20 — **re-verify before applying or flying.**  
**Freshness:** 90 days.

## Taiwan travelers — visa / K-ETA

- Korea operates **K-ETA** (Korea Electronic Travel Authorization) for eligible visa-free travelers. Eligibility and exemptions **change**.
- **Action:** Check only the official site before any application: [https://www.k-eta.go.kr](https://www.k-eta.go.kr) (`src-keta`).
- Do **not** rely on blog posts claiming “K-ETA abolished / not needed” without confirming on the official portal the same week you apply.
- Secondary portal: [Hi Korea](https://www.hikorea.go.kr) (`src-hikorea`).

**Status:** Confirmed that official portals are the source of truth; **current personal eligibility = Decision Required / re-check** (not asserted here).

## Arrival card / immigration

- Forms and e-procedures change. Follow instructions provided by the airline and immigration officers on arrival day.
- Source starting points: Hi Korea / K-ETA help pages.

## Customs

- Check prohibited / restricted items on [Korea Customs](https://www.customs.go.kr) (`src-customs-kr`).
- Food, medication, drones, and large quantities of cosmetics can have special rules — verify before packing.

## Passport validity

- Airlines and immigration may require validity beyond travel dates (commonly discussed as ~6 months — **confirm with your airline and official entry rules**, do not treat this sentence as a hard legal quote).

## Travel insurance

- Strongly recommended for a first overseas trip together.
- Buy from a provider you trust; keep policy number **offline only**.
- Taiwan MOFA consular resources: [https://www.bosa.mofa.gov.tw](https://www.bosa.mofa.gov.tw).

## Official emergency / consular

- Korea: **112** police, **119** fire/medical (`src-emergency-kr` / MOIS).
- Taipei Mission in Korea: [https://www.roc-taiwan.org/kr](https://www.roc-taiwan.org/kr) — copy phone numbers into Emergency Pack at T-7 after re-check.
