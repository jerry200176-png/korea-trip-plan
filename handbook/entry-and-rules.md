## Arrival / special entry banners

The official portal currently shows a **Special Entry Procedure Declaration** banner stating foreigners may need to report entry in certain disaster/special situations. Treat as **Stale-risk / re-check on travel week** — follow the notice linked from the portal, not third-party summaries.

## Customs

- Check prohibited / restricted items on [Korea Customs](https://www.customs.go.kr) (`src-customs-kr`).
- Food, medication, drones, and large quantities of cosmetics can have special rules — verify before packing.

## Passport validity

- Airlines and immigration may require validity beyond travel dates — **confirm with your airline and official entry rules**; this handbook does not hard-code a month count as law.

## Travel insurance

- Strongly recommended for a first overseas trip together.
- Buy from a provider you trust; keep policy number **offline only**.
- Taiwan MOFA consular resources: [https://www.bosa.mofa.gov.tw](https://www.bosa.mofa.gov.tw).
