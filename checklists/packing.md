# Packing checklist

Prioritize comfort and weather safety for a likely **March** trip (Assumption). Re-check forecast at T-7.

## Documents & devices

- [ ] Passport
- [ ] Phone + charger + power bank
- [ ] Instant camera + film (keep film away from extreme heat/cold)
- [ ] Offline maps + handbook PDFs
- [ ] Cards + limited cash

## Clothes (comfort > appearance)

- [ ] Layers for cool March days / wind at coast
- [ ] Waterproof outer layer
- [ ] Comfortable shoes (primary) + backup
- [ ] Socks that prevent blisters
- [ ] Outfit suitable for hanbok rental shop rules (confirm per shop)

## Daily bag

- [ ] Tissue / wet wipes
- [ ] Portable phone strap / small crossbody
- [ ] Reusable shopping bag (cosmetics volume)

## Do not pack

- Items banned by Korea Customs / airline liquid rules without checking official lists
