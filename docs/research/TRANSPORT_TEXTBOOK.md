## B. Seoul local transit

- **T-money:** buy/charge at convenience stores & station channels (VisitKorea + T-money operator). Tap on / tap off.  
- **Metro:** Seoul Metro English portal for maps/user guides; check **last trains nightly** in live tools — do not freeze one clock here.  
- Elevators / escalators / luggage: prefer elevator-marked paths; do not plan critical locks around unverified lockers.  
- **Naver Map** primary; **Kakao Map** backup; Google Maps transit detail in Korea is often weaker — treat as secondary.  
- Taxi = Rescue for rain, late, feet, luggage — not every hop.  
- Keep lodging name/address in Korean for display.
