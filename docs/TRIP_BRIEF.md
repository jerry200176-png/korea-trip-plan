## Planning style

- Prefer clear advance plans; avoid day-of improvisation as the default.
- Avoid overpacking the day and long queues (no 2-hour waits for “must-see” spots).
- Food is a core part of the trip.
- Photos matter (~80% couple shots) but are not the only goal.
- Carry iPhone + Instant camera (Polaroid-style).

## Experience goals

- Beach / nature scenery
- Hanbok experience
- Korean fortune-telling (사주/타로 etc. — candidates TBD)
- GOT7-related experiences (verify still exists before booking)
- Shopping: Alien (Toy Story / 三眼怪) merch, cosmetics, clothes
- Local food including pork soup / 豬肉湯飯類; spicy OK
- No alcohol
- No jjimjilbang (汗蒸幕)
- Avoid restaurants where crustacean seafood is the main focus
- Public transit only (no rental car)
- Limit walking / transfer intensity (feet tire easily)
- Lodging: clean, comfortable, double bed; bathtub optional; convenience store nearby is a plus
- Practical budget; spend more when it clearly improves the experience
